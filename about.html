hello! this is me.

My name is Inna, I live in Haifa, Israel, and making photographs for people.

I’ve been doing photography since the age of 14 when I first touched the old Soviet film camera Zenith that I inherited from my lovely Grandfather. I lost my mind.

I had a chance to work on football matches, civil protests, charity and many more. In my professional life, I’ve been working as the Photo Editor for various media resources around the world. Never give up on ordinary photo shooting of beautiful places and interesting people.

If you want your own experience, I’ll do my best. Just click on “contact” – and we’ll talk.
